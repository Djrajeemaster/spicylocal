Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-14

1) Data We Collect

  • Account data: username, email.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, searches, device info.
  • Content: deals, comments, messages.
  • Location (if allowed): to show nearby deals.
  • Cookies/localStorage: preferences, session state.

2) How We Use Data

  • Operate the Service (auth, posting, moderation).
  • Personalize content and sort deals.
  • Analytics and performance improvements.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and abuse handling.
  • Notifications you opt into (e.g., category alerts).

3) Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We share with service providers (hosting, analytics) under contract.
  • We may disclose when required by law or to protect safety and rights.

4) Your Choices

  • Update or delete your account in the Profile section.
  • Control notifications by category in Settings.
  • Opt out of location sharing from your device settings.

5) Data Security & Retention

We use reasonable safeguards. Retention depends on account status and legal requirements. You can request deletion via Contact.

6) Children

The Service is not intended for users under 13.

7) Changes

We’ll update this Policy as needed and post the new date above.
